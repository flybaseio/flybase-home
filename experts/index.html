The FIT Stack is our rapid MVP stack which stands for:

  • Flybase for the backend
  • Interface for the frontend, usually Angular.js
  • Thin servers - usually in node.js for any extra work that may be needed. (such as twilio processing, etc). If we even need it, a lot of projects such as SideGig or Kilo can be built with just the F and I part of the FIT Stack
